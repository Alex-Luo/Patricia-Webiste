Enaging The Mystery

For those who wish to enter the Great Change with curiosity, deep love and profound understanding. This course helps to awaken awareness of the path before us at the close of life on earth. It is a rich and dynamic experience. Please join me for this exhilarating and deeply sacred Journey into the depths of the unknown.

Creating A Living Tapestry Of Light

This workshop is for families preparing for an upcoming passage of a loved one. I guide families through the preparation and answer questions so that they feel ready to embark on this sacred journey.

Opening To Soul Passage Midwifery

For those who wish to embark on the journey to practicing the work of Soul Passage Midwifery. If you feel called to this extraordinary endeavor, please join Patricia. Prerequisite for apprentice course.
